Portuguese footballer famous for his goal-scoring prowess and athleticism. He has won major titles with clubs like Real Madrid and Manchester United and led Portugal to Euro 2016 and the 2019 Nations League.

Paulo Dybala is an Argentine footballer known for his skillful play and creativity. He has excelled with clubs like Juventus and AS Roma, and is a key player for the Argentine national team.

Mauro Icardi

Mauro Icardi is an Argentine striker known for his goal-scoring ability. He has played for clubs like Inter Milan and Paris Saint-Germain and is recognized for his sharp finishing and movement in the box.

Di Maria

Ángel Di María is an Argentine winger renowned for his speed and crossing ability. He has achieved success with clubs like Real Madrid and PSG and played a key role in Argentina's 2021 Copa América victory.

Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé is a French forward known for his speed and finishing. He has won major titles with Paris Saint-Germain and was instrumental in France's 2018 World Cup win.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian forward celebrated for his speed and goal-scoring. He has achieved success with Liverpool, including winning the Premier League and Champions League.

Harry Kane

Harry Kane is an English striker known for his goal-scoring and playmaking. He has excelled with Tottenham Hotspur and the England national team.

Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian midfielder known for his exceptional passing and vision. He has been a key player for Manchester City, winning multiple Premier League titles and domestic cups.

Philippe Coutinho

Philippe Coutinho is a Brazilian midfielder known for his dribbling and creativity. He has played for clubs like Liverpool and Barcelona and is recognized for his skillful playmaking.
